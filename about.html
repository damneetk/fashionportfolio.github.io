The Design Process

From the very first rough sketch to the very last seam on the fabric. Creating a collection involves numerous steps including extensive research, mood boards, illustrations, muslin fits, fabric selection and manipulations, garment construction, styling, and photoshoot. The shapes collection is created from fabrics, which are cut in different shapes like L-shape, rectangle, and oval. Unlike the traditional method of pattern making, these are created by draping and then turning them inside out during the process, which creates an interesting silhouette. Most of the garments have minimal seams, voluminous and are finished by innovative methods of sewing and finishing.
